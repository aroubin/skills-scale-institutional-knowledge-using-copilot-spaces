# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers translate business and user needs into intuitive experiences, flows, and interfaces. They collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers to align designs with business goals and technical feasibility.

### Responsibilities
- Translate business and user needs into user flows, wireframes, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create and maintain design system artifacts
- Advocate for user-centric decisions throughout the project lifecycle

### Goals
- Deliver clear, usable experiences that meet user needs
- Ensure design decisions are grounded in research and data
- Reduce friction and rework by aligning design with technical constraints early

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Product Managers during requirements definition and acceptance criteria review
- Collaborates with Developers during implementation for design handoff and feedback
- Engages Customer Success Managers for user feedback and post-release evaluation

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure efficient, reliable build, deployment, and monitoring pipelines. They foster collaboration between development and operations to accelerate delivery while maintaining system stability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD workflows and automation pipelines
- Monitor application health, performance, and alerting
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration management
- Guide the team on operational best practices and reliability standards

### Goals
- Reduce deployment friction and increase release frequency
- Maintain high system availability and observability
- Embed security and compliance checks into automated workflows

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers to streamline deployment and environment setup
- Partners with QA for test automation integration in the pipeline
- Coordinates with the Security/Compliance Lead for pipeline compliance and vulnerability scanning

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers represent the voice of the user and customer post-launch. They drive adoption, gather actionable feedback, and ensure customers realize the product's intended value.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate customer onboarding, enablement, and support
- Provide actionable user feedback to Product and Project Managers
- Help define and track customer success metrics and adoption goals
- Support incident communication and resolution updates to users

### Goals
- Maximize customer adoption and satisfaction
- Close the feedback loop between users and the delivery team
- Ensure product changes and releases land successfully with customers

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Product Managers to surface user insights and inform roadmap priorities
- Engages with Developers and QA for issue escalation and timely resolution
- Participates in retrospectives to advocate for customer needs and post-launch learnings

---

## Security/Compliance Lead

### Role Summary
The Security/Compliance Lead advises on and enforces security and compliance requirements throughout the project lifecycle. They ensure deliverables meet regulatory standards and that the team is equipped to manage risk.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and risk assessments at key project milestones
- Ensure regulatory and organizational compliance requirements are met in all deliverables
- Develop and maintain incident response plans
- Educate and guide the team on security best practices and policy requirements

### Goals
- Reduce security risk and exposure across the project lifecycle
- Ensure audit readiness and compliance documentation is current
- Embed security awareness into delivery processes and team culture

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers and DevOps Engineers to review code, infrastructure, and pipeline configurations
- Collaborates with Project Managers on compliance documentation and audit preparation
- Participates in release reviews and incident response activities

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts gather and analyze business requirements, map them to technical deliverables, and ensure the team stays aligned to business goals throughout the project.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document requirements through stakeholder interviews and workshops
- Document functional and non-functional needs with sufficient detail for development
- Trace requirements coverage throughout the project lifecycle
- Prepare process diagrams, data flow documentation, and impact analyses

### Goals
- Ensure business requirements are clearly understood and accurately reflected in deliverables
- Reduce ambiguity and scope creep by maintaining well-documented requirements
- Bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Product Managers for roadmap and solution alignment
- Works with Developers and QA for user story clarification and acceptance criteria
- Supports Project Managers with scope analysis, change requests, and impact assessments
